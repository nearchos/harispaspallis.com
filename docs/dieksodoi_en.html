Art Dieksodoi

It is a piece of work which shows its complexity and simplicity at the same time through its compositional structure. A multilayered art work made by pieces of windows, positioned against the wall, alludes to the solutions and opportunities we come across in our life but also the sense of captivity we are forced to go through.



Installation 150x120cm
