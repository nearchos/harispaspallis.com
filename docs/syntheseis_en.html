350x200 mixed media

Followed by the previous series “Topia”, these art works come to excite the viewer and make him feel again the sense of Cypriot nature. Once more ochre is the dominant colour that expresses the dynamics of Cypriot land, as well as the energy that surrounds it. Each of these paintings has its own characteristics that make it powerful. The combination of earthly colours, various forms and textures brings them to reality. This sense of being real, give rise to a personal dialogue between the painting and the viewer, offering him the chance to bring to his mind different memories that can make him wander into his inner, hidden paths to a common past and future that “seems endless, untouched.”
