Layered fabrics, used clothes, hang form the ceiling like dried animal skins, painted white, in a symbolic act of purification, signifying the adventures, undergone by both flesh and soul, in search of a liberating catharsis. This is entailed in the risky and crucial question whether to keep your individuality or to abolish it and merge with the crowd.
