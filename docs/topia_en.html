Art Topia


220x170, mixed media

Through his work, the artist is trying to capture the quintessence of Cypriot nature. By means of an ochreous colour,he is expressing the dynamics of the harvested fields as well as the energy that surrounds them. The blue line on the top used as the horizon, leads the audience towards "catharsis": it helps them walk through their memory path towards the common past and future that looks "the same, endless, untouched".
