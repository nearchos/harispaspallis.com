Art Visual Ways

Without any thoughts, without any mercy, without any shame they have cruelly built those walls. Those walls have left us outside of our boarders, out of the boarders of our soul, our countries, and our inner world. They are the limitations that other countries have put with force on us. Social, cultural, national, economical limits which control our lives. The people are not free as they would like to be or they think they are, as their freedom depends on the economical powers. As soon as people realize they are surrounded by these invisible walls, they feel imprisoned. They never saw it coming, there were no signs. Out of the sudden, they faced the cruel reality. The walls, the boundaries were always there but they have never took a moment to see close enough. Now, after they got more mature from all the difficulties, can understand what is really happening around the world. The walls are now visible.
